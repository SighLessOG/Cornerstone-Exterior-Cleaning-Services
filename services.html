House Soft Washing

Service 01

House Soft Washing

The number one thing New England homes need. Our humid climate and heavy tree cover grow mold, mildew, and green algae fast — especially on north-facing and shaded siding. Pressure washing siding can cause real damage: blown seals, cracked boards, water forced behind the panels.

Soft washing uses low pressure and a professional-grade cleaning solution to kill what's growing at the root and rinse it away safely. Works on vinyl, wood, stucco, brick, and fiber cement (Hardie board). The result is a deeper clean that lasts far longer than pressure alone.

What's included

  • Full exterior soft wash — all siding surfaces
  • Plant and landscaping protection before we start
  • Window and trim rinse
  • Post-wash walkthrough with you

Priced by home size

Standard single-family home. Every house is different — free estimates always.

Deck & Patio Cleaning

Service 03

Deck & Patio Cleaning

Decks, porches, and patios take constant weather exposure — mold, algae, pollen, and dirt ground into the surface season after season. We clean every type with the right approach: lower pressure and careful technique on wood to protect the grain, soft washing on composite and Trex, and the correct treatment for brick, bluestone, and flagstone patios.

A properly cleaned deck looks years younger and is ready for staining or sealing if you want to take the next step.

What's included

  • Surface assessment — wood, composite, or stone
  • Full deck, porch, and patio surface clean
  • Railings, steps, and joints detailed
  • Post-clean inspection and rinse

Priced by size & condition

Fence Washing

Vinyl and wood fences both collect mold, algae, and grime fast — especially on the shaded side. A good cleaning brings them back dramatically. We adjust pressure and chemical based on the material: vinyl can handle more, while wood gets low pressure and careful treatment to avoid raising the grain.

Gutter Brightening

Service 06

Gutter Brightening

Those black streaks running down the outside face of your gutters are called tiger stripes — oxidation and grime that doesn't rinse off with water alone. We soft wash the exterior face of your gutters and fascia with a solution that removes them cleanly, without harming the paint or finish.

Need the insides cleared out too? We offer interior gutter cleaning separately — debris scooped, downspouts flushed. Ask us to add it when you book.

What's included

  • Chemical treatment of gutter face and fascia
  • Soft wash rinse — no pressure on the gutters
  • Siding rinse below the gutter line

Priced by linear foot

A fast, high-impact add-on — best bundled with a full house wash.
