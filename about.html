About Us

Built on the right foundation.

Cornerstone is a Christian-owned exterior cleaning business in Worcester, MA. Here's who we are, why the name matters, and what you can count on when you hire us.

Why “Cornerstone”

The name isn't accidental.

A cornerstone is the first stone set in a foundation — the one every other stone is aligned to. It determines whether the structure stands straight or leans. We named this company Cornerstone because we believe what's underneath a business matters more than what's on the surface.

For us, that foundation is Christ. That's not a marketing angle — it's the actual reason we do this work the way we do.

"For no one is able to lay a foundation other than the one which is laid, which is Jesus Christ."

— 1 Corinthians 3:11 (LSB)

We're a Christian-owned business. That means we take seriously the idea that there's no corner of life — including work done with your hands — that falls outside the lordship of Christ. The Dutch theologian Abraham Kuyper put it plainly: "There is not a square inch in the whole domain of our human existence over which Christ does not cry: Mine!" That includes a driveway. It includes a house wash. It includes showing up to your home and treating it with respect.

We don't do good work to impress customers or build a reputation. We do it because we answer to someone higher than a customer review — and because loving your neighbor isn't suspended when you pick up a pressure washer. The Great Commandment reaches into ordinary work. Showing up when we said we would, handling your property carefully, leaving things better than we found them — that's not just professionalism. It's how we serve the people God has placed around us.

"Let the favor of the Lord our God be upon us; and confirm for us the work of our hands — yes, confirm the work of our hands."

— Psalm 90:17 (LSB)

Our Story

A local business, built close to home.

Cornerstone was founded in Worcester, MA by Silas. After years in field service and industrial maintenance — diagnosing machines, solving problems, and doing hands-on technical work — Silas started Cornerstone to build something closer to home: a local business that serves the Worcester area with skill, integrity, and genuine care for the people he works for.

He's your neighbor, not a franchise. And he takes your property seriously because he'd want the same care taken with his own.

What You Can Expect

The standard we hold ourselves to.

We show up on time. No vague all-day windows. We tell you when we'll be there, and we're there.

We communicate clearly. Before, during, and after the job — you always know where things stand.

We protect your property. Plants covered, cars kept clear, nothing left damaged or dripping.

We don't leave until it's right. No rushing to the next job. If it doesn't look right, we fix it before we go.

If something isn't right, we make it right. No argument, no runaround. That's the guarantee.

We treat your home like our own. Because the work is worth doing well — not just well enough.

Our Process

What working with us actually looks like.

From your first message to the finished job — here's exactly what to expect, step by step.

1

You reach out

Fill out the quote form, call, or text. We respond fast — usually the same day, often within the hour. We'll ask a few quick questions about your property and what you're looking for.

2

You get a quote

For most jobs we can give you an accurate price from photos or satellite — no trip needed. For larger or more complex jobs we'll come take a look first so the number is right. Either way, free, no obligation.

3

We confirm and schedule

Once you approve the price we'll lock in a date and send you a written agreement covering exactly what's included. We'll also let you know what to do before we arrive — close windows, move vehicles, that kind of thing.

4

Job day

We show up on time, walk the property before touching anything, and take before photos. We protect your plants, cover any sensitive fixtures, and get to work. Most jobs are done in a few hours. We clean up completely before we leave.

5

Walkthrough and payment

When the job's done we walk the property with you and make sure you're happy before anything else. Payment is due on completion — cash, check, or Venmo. If anything isn't right, we fix it before we leave.

6

The follow-up

After the job we send you a complete summary — the services performed, before and after photos, and anything we noticed on the property you should know about. You'll always know exactly what was done and why.

Every job follows this process — no shortcuts, no surprises. If something unexpected comes up mid-job, we stop and talk to you before doing anything that wasn't agreed on.
